About Me

Franca Nwakaego Chukwuemeka is a certified HR professional with cross-industry experience spanning consulting at Pruvia Integrated Ltd, oil and gas at Gul Treasures Ltd, and the securetech and telecoms environment at SB Telecoms & Devices Ltd.

Her work sits at the point where people, performance, and business growth meet. She has supported business owners with workforce planning, talent decisions, employee engagement, performance conversations, career development, and practical HR systems that help organizations operate with more clarity and confidence.

Franca brings a balanced perspective to every engagement: a strong grasp of people management, a coach's eye for potential, and a modern interest in how artificial intelligence can make HR more strategic, fair, and future-ready.

Certification & Education

Franca holds a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science and Public Administration from the University of Benin, Nigeria. She further strengthened her people-management foundation through studies in Industrial Relations and Personnel Management at the University of Lagos, Nigeria, and advanced her global business perspective with a qualification in International Business with Human Resources Management from Ulster University, United Kingdom.

Her professional credibility is also supported by active membership in respected HR bodies. She is an Associate Member of CIPM Nigeria and an Associate Member of CIPD, United Kingdom, reflecting her commitment to continuous learning, ethical HR practice, and internationally relevant people-management standards.
